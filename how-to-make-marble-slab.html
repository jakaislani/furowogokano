    Discover what you need to do to create Marble Slabs in LEGO Fortnite.

    First Published December 15, 2023, 13:17

    LEGO Fortnite has made a huge splash with gamers. In this survival sandbox, players will have to focus on crafting a variety of different items to survive in the game world. Crafting is the most important aspect of the game and it will be the key to unlocking a variety of content in LEGO Fortnite.

    For example, it allows you to travel faster and build the tools needed to survive. But there are some materials that are harder to get than others, like the Marble Slab. Here's how craft Marble Slabs in LEGO Fortnite.

    How To Craft Marble Slabs In LEGO Fortnite

    Marble Slab is not something that you can naturally find in LEGO Fortnite. They will have to craft it using the variety of tools that are available to them. To craft Marble Slabs, you will first need to craft a Stone Breaker, which requires 20 Knotroot and 35 Marble to craft it.

    Once that's done, you can craft as many Marble Slabs as they want. Marble Slabs are essential crafting materials. They can be used to craft an upgraded Crafting Bench or for making bigger treasure chests. So getting the Stone Breaker as soon as possible is important in LEGO Fortnite.

    How To Find Marble In LEGO Fortnite

    Of course, you can't get Marble Slabs without first finding Marble. To do so, head into caves to find what you need. From there, you can mine as much Marble as you can cerry to make a variety of tools and equipment.

    But mining Marble at the beginning will be difficult. You'll first need to craft an Uncommon Pickaxe to do so. To make an Uncommon Pickaxe, you'll need to collect three Knotroot Rods and 3 Bones. Get the Uncommon Pickaxe, mine the Marble, make the Stone Breaker, and then you can craft Marble Slabs.
