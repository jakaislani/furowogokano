    Koo Hye-sun Facts

  • She made her first theatrical film appearance as a Girl on Couch in the 2007 American musical drama film, August Rush, starring Freddie Highmore, Keri Russell, Jonathan Rhys Meyers, Terrence Howard, Alex O’Loughlin, Robin Williams, and William Sadler.
  • She brought to life the character of Queen Je-heon/Yoon So-hwa in the historical drama series The King and I in 2007. This performance won her the “New Star Award” at the SBS Drama Awards in 2007.
  • In 2009, Koo Hye-sun played Geum Jan-di in the romantic comedy-drama series Boys Over Flowers. For this role, she won the 4th Andre Kim Best Star Award for “Best Female Star” and KBS Drama Awards for “Excellence Award, Actress in a Mid-length Drama” and “Best Couple Award” (with Lee Min-ho).
  • As a director, she has worked on films such as The Peach Tree, Daughter, Magic, Dark Yellow, and Mystery Pink.
  • Koo Hye-sun released her first album, Breath, in 2009.
  • She is an avid fan of the baseball team SK Wyverns.
  • Koo Hye-sun underwent polyp removal surgery in September 2019.
  • She is a fan of Lee Juck, a singer, lyricist, composer, and arranger.
  • Koo Hye-sun enjoys swimming and can also play the piano.
  • She has 2 chihuahuas named Soondae and Goonbam and a golden retriever named Gamja. She also has a cat named Mango.
  • In November 2021, it was revealed that she had signed an exclusive contract with IOK Company. In December 2023, she renewed her contract with the agency.
