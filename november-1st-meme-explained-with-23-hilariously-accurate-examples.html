    It’s that time of year again! First. we were counting down the days until Halloween and now it’s over we are gearing up for Christmas!

    Even though it feels like it was just summer, Halloween has come and gone and it’s now time to buy a winter coat and dust off those Christmas decorations as the festive season is upon us.

    We have rounded up the best memes to represent the transition from spooky season to the festive season!

    What are November 1st memes?

    November 1st memes refer to those individuals who have packed their Halloween costumes into storage for another year and have dusted off their Christmas decorations at the same time.

    Memes have taken over Twitter referencing those who believe that the festive season begins at midnight once Halloween is over, so get ready for some festive memes.

    Mariah Carey declares the end of Halloween and welcomes Christmas

    Mariah Carey officially declared the end of the spooky season by posting a video to Instagram at midnight on Halloween.

    In the Instagram video, the singer’s doorstep is shown covered in Halloween decorations including three pumpkins that say: “It’s not time.”

    When the clock strikes midnight, the 52-year-old smashes the pumpkins to pieces with a candy-striped stick as the opening of All I Want for Christmas Is You starts to play.
