    Baked Bacon Macaroni and Cheese

    Classic Mac â€˜N Cheese gets a grown-up twist with this Baked Bacon Macaroni and Cheese! Crunchy bacon and caramelized onions take this comforting food to the next level of awesomeness!

    Course Entree Cuisine American Keyword baked bacon macaroni and cheese, mac and cheese Prep Time 5 minutes minutes Cook Time 45 minutes minutes Total Time 50 minutes minutes Servings 12 Calories 493kcal Author Trish - Mom On Timeout

    Ingredients

    • 2 onions sliced
    • 7 tbsp butter divided
    • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
    • 3 3/4 cups whole milk
    • 1 tsp ground mustard
    • 1 pinch cayenne
    • 3 cups shredded sharp cheddar
    • 1 cup shredded Gruyere
    • 1 lb elbow macaroni cooked to al dente
    • 8 slices bacon cooked and crumbled
    • 1/4 cup Italian-style bread crumbs

    Instructions

    • Preheat oven to 350F.

    • Caramelize onions in 2 tablespoons of butter until golden. Remove and set aside.

    • Melt 4 tablespoons of butter in pan over low heat. Add flour and whisk until smooth.

    • Continue cooking for 3-4 minutes.

    • Gradually whisk in milk, mustard, and cayenne.

    • Bring mixture to a simmer and continue cooking, stirring often, for additional 5-10 minutes until slightly thickened.

    • Remove from heat.

    • Stir in cheeses until fully melted.

    • Stir in macaroni, onion, and finally stir in the bacon.

    • Melt remaining tablespoon of butter in a small dish and stir in bread crumbs.

    • Sprinkle over the top of the macaroni and cheese.

    • Bake uncovered for approximately 20 minutes or until slightly browned and bubbling. Let cool for 5 minutes before serving.
