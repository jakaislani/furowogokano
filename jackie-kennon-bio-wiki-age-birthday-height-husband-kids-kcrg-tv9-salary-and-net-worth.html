    Jackie Kennon KCRG-TV9 News

    On 10th March 2020, Kennon named co-anchor for KCRG-TV9 morning newscasts

    Kennon began her role, joining Nicole Agee and Kaj O’Mara(meteorologist) as part of the weekday morning team for the First News, Early Morning News, and Morning News. It is her second stint in the KCRG-TV9 newsroom.

    Kennon joined the station while earning her degree at Wartburg College and returned in June 2019 to co-anchor the weekend evening newscasts. Before returning to KCRG-TV9, she reported and anchored at KTVO-TV which serves viewers in Ottumwa and Kirksville, Missouri.
