    Bharti Singh is an stand-up comedian from Amritsar, Punjab, India. Her father is of Nepali descent, whereas her Mom is a Punjabi. Bharti’s father died in her childhood. She has two siblings. She got married to famous model and writer Harsh Limbachiyaa on 3rd December 2017.

    She was the second runner-up of a popular comedy reality show “The Great Indian Laughter Challenge Season 4” aired on STAR One, for which she got acclaim for her off-the-cuff comedy child role named Lalli. Bharti Singh also starred as a contestant alongside Paresh Ganatra and Sharad Kelkar in Comedy Circus 3 ka Tadka as well as Comedy Circus Mahasangram along with her team from Comedy Circus Ke Superstars with Paresh Ganatra, Comedy Circus 3 Ka Tadka, Comedy Circus Ka Jadoo. In the 2011, Bharti Singh also starred in Jubilee Comedy Circus, Comedy Circus Ka Naya Daur and Comedy Circus Ke Taansen aired on Sony Entertainment Television. Besides this, she hosted Comedy Nights Bachao together with Krishna Abhishek.

    In the same year, Bharti Singh appeared in a Television series titled “Pyaar Mein Twist” aired on STAR Plus and afterward acted as a participant on celebrity dancing reality show titled Jhalak Dikhhla Jaa 5. In the year 2012, she hosted TV show “Sau Saal Cinema Ke” along with famous actors like Ragini Khanna, Shruti Ulfat and Karan Tacker. Bharti Singh also starred as a guest star in MasterChef India Season 3. She also starred as a guest star in dancing show Nach Baliye 6.

    Bharti has also hosted a succession of Indian talent shows like India’s Got Talent 5, India’s Got Talent 6 and India’s Got Talent 7. In 2018, she hosted India’s Got Talent 8 aired on Colors TV and afterward starred in comedy drama “The Kapil Sharma Show” as Titli Yadav. In 2019, Bharti Singh participated in “Khatron Ke Khiladi 9” aired on Colors TV together with Harsh again.
