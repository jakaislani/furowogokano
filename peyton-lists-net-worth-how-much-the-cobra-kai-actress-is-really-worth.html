    Cobra Kai star Peyton List has been acting since she was a child, and it has certainly paid off for her. According to Celebrity Net Worth, List is worth around $7 million, which is pretty great for a woman who celebrated her 22nd birthday in 2020. In addition to acting, List is already diversifying her revenue streams by dabbling in music, too. According to Fashionista, she released a pop single called "Liar Liar" in 2018 and another titled "Don't Cry" in 2019. Though she's certainly got some disposable income, the young star said she's careful not to go overboard, particularly when it comes to her wardrobe.

    "I usually only try to buy one or two things when I go out shopping," she told Fashionista. "I try to keep my closet more limited [to a] small amount of things that I really love.
