    Here's why Devil in Ohio's Marci T House is so familiar

    Were all used to seeing performers were familiar with on our screens but were not always able to pinpoint where exactly weve seen them before. When it doesnt come to you straight away, its always handy to reach for the phone and investigate.

    10 May 2024, 00:00

    We’re all used to seeing performers we’re familiar with on our screens but we’re not always able to pinpoint where exactly we’ve seen them before.

    When it doesn’t come to you straight away, it’s always handy to reach for the phone and investigate.

    There are a number of instances where you may find yourselves doing just that when watching Devil in Ohio, the American mystery thriller that hit Netflix on Friday, September 2nd 2022.

    Created by Daria Polatin and based on her book of the same name, it tells the tale of a psychiatrist who cares for a patient that escaped from a cult.

    While checking it out, you’ll want to remind yourselves of what Marci T House has been in…

    Marci T House stars in Devil in Ohio

    The character of Adele Thornton is portrayed by Marci T House and the 50-year-old American actress will be recognisable to viewers for a wealth of different reasons.

    She has starred in a number of other recent Netflix projects including Ivy + Bean (Charlotte), Julie and the Phantoms (Mrs Harrison) and Midnight Mass (Mainland Doctor).

    On the other hand, some may know her from the likes of Tribal (Victoria Mann), Day of the Dead (Captain Gloria Pike), Chesapeake Shores (Hannah Urso), iZombie (Lieutenant Devore), and Timeless (Michelle).

    Looking ahead, she’s set to be in the Netflix movie Lou (Agent Lee) released on Friday, September 23rd 2022 and the TV movie Monster High: The Movie (Bloodgood).

    Is Marci T House on Instagram?

    Yes, you can find the actress on Instagram at sleepwalker413.

    She recently posted to celebrate the Devil in Ohio release day and suggested: “maybe binge this one after the kids go to bed.”

    Very sound advice.

    Devil in Ohio cast

    Marci is joined by a central ensemble of other gifted performers. Check out some names and their respective roles below:

    • Emily Deschanel as Dr. Suzanne Mathis
    • Sam Jaeger as Peter Mathis
    • Gerardo Celasco as Detective Lopez
    • Madeleine Arthur as Mae
    • Xaria Dotson as Jules Mathis
    • Alisha Newton as Helen Mathis
    • Naomi Tan as Dani Mathis
    • Bradley Stryker as Sheriff Wilkins
    • Jason Sakaki as Isaac Kimura

    Devil in Ohio is streaming exclusively on Netflix.
