    Henry Cavill Bio, Age, Wife, Children, Parents, Career, Movies, Net Worth: – Henry Cavill, officially known as Henry William Dalgliesh Cavill is a famous British actor.

    He’s also a fitness freak and a famous socialite. Henry William Dalgliesh Cavill gained widespread popularity after playing the character of Superman in the DC movies. He’s English by Nationality and has his belief in the Roman Catholic church (Christianity).

    Henry Cavill Biography

    Henry William Dalgliesh Cavill was born on May 5, 1983, in Saint Helier, Jersey. He’s best known for his portrayal of Charles Brandon in Showtime’s The Tudors and in DC Comics character Superman in the DC Extended Universe.

    Henry Cavill also made an appearance as Geralt of Rivia in the Netflix fantasy series The Witcher, and Sherlock Holmes in the Netflix film Enola Holmes and its 2022 sequel.

    Henry Cavill Career

    Henry Cavill started his career with roles in the feature adaptations of The Count of Monte Cristo and I Capture The Castle. He later portrayed supporting roles in several television series, including BBC’s The Inspector Lynley Mysteries, ITV’s Midsomer Murders, and Showtime’s The Tudors.
